Ray Marching...! What an interesting technique!

Two days ago, I was binge-watching youtube videos all day, and
an interesting thumbnail of one video clip caught my eyes.
The title was "Ray Marching, and making 3D Worlds with Math".
The idea that triangles are not the building blocks of an object in 3D world was really fascinating.
I have always thought that a triangle is the foundation of all objects when drawing a 3d scene.
Meshes, Vertices, triangles and other stuff that I thought was mandatory for 3d game development are not required in Ray Marching.
All that is needed in Ray Marching is a signed distance function
to calculate a distance between my camera location and a location of an object.
I am still very new to this technology and still try to grasp the idea, wishing one day I apply thie technique to my future projects.
